Research Areas

Horticultural Crop Genomics

Genome assembly, annotation, and comparative genomics of horticultural crops including tomato, watermelon, melon, cucumber, spinach, rose, kiwifruit, apple, and chestnut.

Multi-omics Data Integration

Integration of genomics, transcriptomics, epigenomics, and metabolomics data to understand gene regulatory networks in horticultural crops and to power genome-based breeding.

Bioinformatics Tools & Databases

Development of widely-used bioinformatics tools (iTAK, VirusDetect, iAssembler) and crop-specific genomics databases (CuGenDB, SpinachBase, KGD, HSE, and more).
